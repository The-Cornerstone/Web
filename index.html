We are The Cornerstone.
Open-source anticheat
research and resources.

There is currently a notable lack of research targeting cheat and anticheat development - it's a black box. We aim to provide resources that help to understand the workings of anticheat and cheat software, as well as current trends and exploitation vectors. This puts pressure on developers to consider the privacy and security implications of their anticheat implementations, and provides smaller developers with resources to aid them in developing robust, secure, and private anticheat software.

Download our proof of concept cheat, view the source, or read our docs.
